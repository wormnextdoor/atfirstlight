MAN FINDS MISSING 16-YEAR-OLD GIRL’S SEVERED HEAD IN WAREHOUSE 

Published Aug. 24, 2024, 12:31 p.m. •  By Morgan Houde 

After entering a warehouse on Cherrier road, Île Bizard, to retrieve supplies, Thomas Gauthier discovered the headless body of 16-year-old River Ahmed inside. 

The girl’s parents had reported her missing on Saturday, which police say is likely when she died. She was last seen at her parents’ house on Saturday morning before she left to visit her friend and never returned. The friend’s identity has not been disclosed; her parents have said that she did not mention the person in question by name. 

According to police, it seems like River broke into the warehouse through the window and was then attacked and decapitated with an axe. The murder weapon has been retrieved by police and is currently undergoing analysis. 

Police are continuing to investigate the occurrence to prevent it from repeating itself. All parties involved declined to comment on the case.
