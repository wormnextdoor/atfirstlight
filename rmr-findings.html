She found me. 

I don’t know how much time I have. Strange things have been happening ever since I found and read the notebooks. Bumps in the house, things moving seemingly on their own, and a constant sense of being watched. Even now, I can feel eyes on me, watching my every move. I heard that Milo and Castel were spotted in the area recently, which has to mean that she’s here. It’s her. She knows that I know. 

I won’t get to finish this, but someone else has to. Everything is in the notebooks. Isaac wasn’t insane or a traitor. He was trying to warn the Gods of a danger only he could see: he called her a “harbinger of the End”, a young woman who kills the Gifted, takes their amulets, then impersonates them. He watched it happen. Sophie Miller, the last of the Miller family to wield an amulet, murdered before his eyes. The amulet of air was taken from the corpse, then the Vertical Eye was drawn on her forehead. The killer had white eyes and a stature taken straight from paintings of Gods among men: she’s not human. She can’t be. She had incredible strength, the kind no human could ever possess, and she transformed, taking Sophie’s appearance, but only two things remained: her injuries, and those white eyes. 

He tried to warn us, but no one listened. They called him crazy. His final notebook explains how he tried to kill her with a curse, a curse that should’ve made her mind implode, but it all went wrong. Who knows what the curse really did, or if it did anything at all. He said that he would try to find out, but there are no more entries after that. It’s said that he killed himself, but I don’t think that’s what happened at all. She’s a thousand times more dangerous than I thought. I don’t know if it’s possible to kill her. A being incapable of dying is unheard of, I know, but if a fatal curse didn’t work, then what will? Can anything be done? I have to warn people.
