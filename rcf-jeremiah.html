INTERVIEW OF JEREMIAH LOWLEY 

BY DET. LEBLANC:  

Q. Good evening, Jeremiah. Have a seat.

A. I didn’t do anything.

Q. Relax. You’re not in trouble. I just wanted to ask you a couple questions.

A. What’s this about?

Q. Do you know River Ahmed?

A. No. No, I’ve got no clue who that is.

Q. Really? I’ve been told that you do.

A. Well, I don’t.

Q. Does this ring a bell?

A. ...Oh, her? Yeah, I met her once. Forgot her name, though. What’d you say it was?

Q. River Ahmed. She got involved in a fight you were in, correct?

A. Yeah.

Q. Tell me more about that.

A. I was walking home after hanging out with my friends and stopped by my old primary school for a drink from the water fountain. Then, as I was leaving, I bumped into this kid I knew in, like, sixth grade. He started cussing me out and then things escalated into a fight. So, he was beating me up cause I didn’t come to fight, I came for water, until this chick showed up and dragged him off me. She threatened him with a knife. That scared him off. But I was all beaten up and bruised, so she took me to her house, and her mom helped me out. So, yeah, she was cool.

Q. This other person involved, do you know his name?

A. Yeah. Castel... uh... I dunno his last name. Something Spanish-sounding.

Q. Castel Reyes?

A. Yeah, yeah, him.

Q. I see. Can you tell me what Castel’s problem with you was?

A. Eh, it’s real old. The man just never moves on. I broke his nose back in primary school, and he gave me a concussion. Got suspended for that. He did, I mean. I didn’t get punished.

Q. Do you recall how that fight initiated?

A. Sorta. He was real close with this other kid that I didn’t really get along with, and got involved in stuff that didn’t concern him. He was throwing all sorts of empty threats around; all bark and no bite, that guy. That’s what I thought, anyway. So, I punched him, thinking it’d scare him off, and the rest is history.

Q. Another thing: did you know Florian Elster?

A. Yeah, that’s the kid I mentioned.

Q. Oh, really? Castel and Florian were close?

A. Mhm. They did everything together. You rarely saw one without the other.

Q. What was your feud with Florian about?

A. I just didn’t like ███ Apparenlty, it’s a crime not to worship ███ the way everyone else did.

Q. Hm, I see. Thank you for time. That’d be all for now.

A. I’m confused. What does Florian have to do with River?

Q. I’m afraid River was murdered some time ago, and Castel disappeared not long after.

A. Oh ████.

Q. Please, if you have any information regarding Castel’s whereabouts or something else that may be helpful with River’s case, don’t hesitate to reach out.

A. Oh, yeah, for sure. Uh, good luck with the, um, investigation.

Q. Thank you. Good night, Jeremiah.

A. ‘Night, officer.
